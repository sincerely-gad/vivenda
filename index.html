Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

What services does Vivenda GC provide?
Vivenda GC specializes in commercial building construction, steel and form works, road and pavement construction, industrial projects, and comprehensive site coordination and project management services.
How long does a typical construction project take?
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Commercial buildings typically take 12-24 months, while road and pavement projects range from 3-12 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.
What quality standards do you follow?
We adhere to international construction standards and Rwanda Building Code requirements. Our quality control systems ensure continuous monitoring, inspection, and compliance throughout every project phase.
How do you manage project budgets and costs?
We provide detailed cost estimates upfront and implement strict budget controls throughout the project. Our team uses value engineering principles to maximize quality while maintaining financial efficiency and transparency.
