About Us

About Us

Building Rwanda's Future

Vivenda General Construction Ltd is a leading construction company based in Kigali, Rwanda, specializing in delivering comprehensive infrastructure solutions across the nation. With over a decade of proven expertise, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in commercial building construction, industrial projects, road and pavement works, and comprehensive project management services.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every project we undertake, from multi-use commercial buildings to large-scale industrial facilities. We combine technical expertise with innovative construction methodologies to deliver projects that meet international quality standards while respecting timelines and budgets.

Our Inspiration

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction and infrastructure solutions that contribute to Rwanda's economic growth and development. We are committed to providing our clients with quality workmanship, innovative solutions, and professional project management that exceeds expectations while maintaining the highest standards of safety and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be Rwanda's most trusted and innovative construction company, recognized for transforming the nation's infrastructure landscape through excellence, integrity, and sustainable building practices. We aspire to set industry benchmarks in quality construction and project delivery while fostering long-term partnerships that drive community development and national progress.
